About Ruth

Front End Web Developer with a goal to create websites to the upmost satisfaction of clients. I am interested in collaborating on projects worldwide remotely.

Born and raised mostly in Canada with 6 years of schooling + 1 year of working in South Korea. Fluent in both English and Korean.

High interest in developing sites to improve accessibility for the vulnerable population.

Currently Toronto based with plans to relocate to Germany or South Korea in the next few years.
